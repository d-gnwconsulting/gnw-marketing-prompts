## What Is GEO and AEO?

**GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)** is the practice of optimizing content to be cited by AI-powered search engines like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Gemini. Unlike traditional SEO, which focuses on ranking in Google's blue links, GEO focuses on being the source AI systems quote when answering user questions.

**AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** is the broader discipline of optimizing content for any answer engine — including Google featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, voice assistants, and Knowledge Panels. Content optimized for AEO tends to also perform well for GEO.

Both disciplines prioritize direct answers, entity clarity, and structured formatting over keyword density.
